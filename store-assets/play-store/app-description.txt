GeoNova — Professional Surveying Platform

Built by a licensed land surveyor for surveyors.

GeoNova removes the computational burden from 
field surveyors so they can focus on what matters:
collecting accurate observations.

FEATURES:
• Complete survey calculation suite
• Traverse adjustment (Bowditch & Transit rule)
• Leveling — Rise & Fall / Height of Collimation
• Horizontal curves — elements and stakeout table
• COGO — radiation, intersection, Tienstra resection
• Missing line, setting out, tacheometry, grade
• Project management with interactive map
• CSV bulk import with preview
• Professional PDF report generation
• DXF export for AutoCAD
• GPS stakeout with audio guidance
• Process field notes — auto-detect survey type
• Offline capable — all calculations work without internet
• UTM coordinate conversion — millimeter accuracy
• Field mode — digital field book on your phone
